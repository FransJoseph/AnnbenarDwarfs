 I24_hearth_and_hold_desc:0 "To build a hold in the Serpentspine requires three necessities in varying degrees. The first is an abundance of resources -- vast quantities of ores and minerals to both support the construction efforts on site, and to export for trade in order to procure any missing materials. Second is a perceived need for an easily defensible home, but with defences well beyond any normal city. The third is time: A proper hold takes decades of planning once the original settlement is constructed. Sewers, ventilation, residential districts, access tunnels for foot and cart traffic, farms, and more. Then, centuries of slowly chiselling away stone, inch by inch, to merely scratch the surface of what one could consider the art of creating a hold.
